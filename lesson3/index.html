Purpose of the Site

This website will unite the business people of Metro City in a new and exciting way. It will allow them to connect with each other and work together to better the economy and reach the general consumer. It will be responsive and provide useful information that will be used to make life easier.



There will be a place they can go to easily communicate with one another so they can coordinate efforts. They can also refer customers to each other and offer each other financial support.


Lastly, this is a place where those job searchers can go to network. They can reach out to the businesses and get more involved in the community.
